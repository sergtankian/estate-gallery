Estate Item Gallery

This website was created to help family members look through items from the estate and indicate if they are interested in something. The goal is to make the process simple and fair so everyone has a chance to see what is available.

Browse the Items

Click the button below to open the gallery of items. Each item has a photo, the room it came from, and its current status.

How to Use the Gallery

  • You can search for items using the search box.
  • You can filter by room to see items from a specific part of the house.
  • Click any photo to view a larger version.

Requesting an Item

If you are interested in an item, click the "I'm Interested" button under the item. This will open a short form where you can submit your name and contact information.

Submitting the form lets us know you are interested, but it does not automatically reserve the item. If multiple people request the same item, we will work it out fairly.

Item Status

  • Available – The item has not yet been claimed.
  • Claimed – Someone has already requested the item and it is no longer available.

Items that are already claimed will appear dimmed and their request button will be disabled.

Thank You

Thank you for taking the time to look through the items. This system helps everyone see what is available and keeps things organized.
