The working theory of this website is for it to be a hosting platform for galleries of items from mom & dad's estate to better fascilitate beloved items getting to their respective new owners, and to help coordinate what has been spoken for and for that matter what is even there waiting to be claimed.

This means the website must include a navigation ui to the various galleries, a users library, and a rudimentary system to "react" to pictures from users so they can make it known they would like that item. Be it in the form of a comment or something simplier.
